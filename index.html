How it works

A simple process for busy engineers & hiring managers

<div class="steps-grid">
  <div class="step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <h3>Quick intro call</h3>
    <p>
      20–30 minutes to clarify what you’re looking for – role, level, salary, timing and any non-negotiables.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <h3>Focused search</h3>
    <p>
      I use an engineering lens to filter CVs, talk through real project work and narrow to a short, realistic list.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <h3>Shortlist & feedback</h3>
    <p>
      You get a concise shortlist with commentary – not just keywords – plus honest feedback from both sides.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
