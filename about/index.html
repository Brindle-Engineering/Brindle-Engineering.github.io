About Brindle Engineering Talent#

My name is Matthew Brindle, an engineer based in Western Australia.

After experiencing the slow and inconsistent engineering recruitment process in WA, I started Brindle Engineering Talent to offer a more technical, accurate and human approach.

I specialise in:

  • Reading engineering CVs properly
  • Understanding real skills, not just buzzwords
  • Helping engineers present themselves clearly
  • Helping employers quickly identify genuine capability

The goal is simple:
engineers shouldn’t be overlooked, and employers shouldn’t struggle to find great engineers.
